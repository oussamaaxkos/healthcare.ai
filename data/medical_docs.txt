Title: NHS - Fever in Adults
Text: Fever is usually a sign that your body is fighting an infection. It can be caused by a variety of illnesses.
Source: https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/fever-in-adults/

Title: NHS - Headache
Text: Headache can be caused by stress, dehydration or other medical conditions. Drink water and rest before seeking medical advice.
Source: https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/headache/

Title: NHS - Common Cold
Text: The common cold is a mild viral infection of the nose, throat, sinuses and upper airways.
Source: https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/common-cold/
